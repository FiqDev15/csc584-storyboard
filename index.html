About DemocraTech

DemocraTech is a comprehensive student election management system developed for Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM). Our platform modernizes the democratic process by providing a secure, efficient, and transparent voting experience for all students.

Built with cutting-edge technology and designed with the student experience in mind, DemocraTech ensures every vote counts and every voice is heard.
