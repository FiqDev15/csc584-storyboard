Getting Started

Welcome to DemocraTech! This guide will help you navigate the student election system.

How to Register

To participate in elections, you need to register with your university credentials:

  1. Click on the Register button from the login page
  2. Fill in your personal and academic information
  3. Use your official university email address
  4. Verify your account via email confirmation

System Requirements

For optimal experience, ensure you have:

  • Latest version of Chrome, Firefox, or Edge
  • Stable internet connection
  • JavaScript enabled in your browser

Voting Guide

How to Vote

Follow these steps to cast your vote:

  1. Log in to your DemocraTech account
  2. Go to "Elections" page from the navigation menu
  3. Find active elections you're eligible to vote in
  4. Click "Vote Now" button for the election
  5. Review candidate information
  6. Select your preferred candidate
  7. Confirm your selection
  8. Submit your vote
Important: Once submitted, votes cannot be changed. Please review your selection before submitting.

Election Schedule

Elections follow a specific schedule:

  • Campaign Period: Candidates campaign
  • Voting Period: Cast your votes
  • Results Announcement: After voting closes
